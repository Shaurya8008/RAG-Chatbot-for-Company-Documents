# Acme Corporation Employee Benefits Overview
**Version 2.3 | Effective Date: January 1, 2025 | Department: Human Resources**
**Access Level: All Employees | Owner: Total Rewards Team**

---

## 1. Benefits Eligibility

All full-time employees (30+ hours per week) are eligible for the full benefits package. Part-time employees working 20-29 hours per week are eligible for limited benefits as noted below. Benefits coverage begins on the first day of the month following your start date.

## 2. Health Insurance

### 2.1 Medical Plans

Acme offers three medical plan options:

| Plan | Monthly Premium (Employee Only) | Monthly Premium (Family) | Deductible | Out-of-Pocket Max |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| PPO Standard | $120 | $380 | $1,500 | $5,000 |
| PPO Premium | $180 | $520 | $750 | $3,000 |
| HDHP with HSA | $80 | $250 | $2,800 | $6,500 |

Acme pays 80% of the premium cost for all plans. The amounts shown above are the employee's portion.

### 2.2 Dental Insurance
- Preventive care covered at 100% (cleanings, x-rays)
- Basic procedures (fillings, extractions) covered at 80%
- Major procedures (crowns, bridges) covered at 50%
- Annual maximum benefit: $2,000 per person
- Employee monthly premium: $25 (individual) / $65 (family)

### 2.3 Vision Insurance
- Annual eye exam covered at 100%
- $200 allowance for frames every 24 months
- Contact lens allowance: $150 per year
- Employee monthly premium: $10 (individual) / $25 (family)

## 3. Retirement Benefits

### 3.1 401(k) Plan
- Acme matches 100% of employee contributions up to 4% of salary
- Additional 50% match on the next 2% of salary (total match potential: 5% of salary)
- Vesting schedule: 25% per year, fully vested after 4 years
- Employees can contribute up to the IRS annual limit
- Roth 401(k) option available
- Enrollment begins after 30 days of employment

### 3.2 Financial Planning
Free access to a certified financial planner for retirement planning consultations, available quarterly through the benefits portal.

## 4. Life and Disability Insurance

### 4.1 Life Insurance
- Basic life insurance: 2x annual salary (company-paid)
- Supplemental life insurance: Available up to 5x annual salary (employee-paid)
- AD&D insurance: Included with basic life insurance

### 4.2 Disability Insurance
- Short-term disability: 60% of salary for up to 26 weeks (company-paid)
- Long-term disability: 60% of salary after 26 weeks (company-paid)
- Waiting period: 14 calendar days for short-term disability

## 5. Wellness Benefits

### 5.1 Wellness Stipend
All full-time employees receive a $100/month wellness stipend that can be used for:
- Gym memberships
- Fitness classes or equipment
- Mental health apps (Calm, Headspace)
- Nutrition coaching
- Ergonomic home office equipment

### 5.2 Employee Assistance Program (EAP)
Free, confidential counseling services available 24/7:
- Up to 8 counseling sessions per year per issue
- Legal consultation (30-minute sessions)
- Financial counseling
- Work-life balance resources
- Crisis support hotline

### 5.3 Mental Health Days
In addition to sick leave, employees may take up to 4 mental health days per year without requiring a doctor's note. These are separate from PTO and sick leave.

## 6. Professional Development

### 6.1 Education Reimbursement
- Up to $5,000 per year for job-related courses, certifications, or degree programs
- Must receive manager approval before enrollment
- Requires a grade of B or better (or Pass for pass/fail courses)
- Reimbursement processed within 30 days of submitting receipts and transcripts

### 6.2 Conference Allowance
Each employee receives up to $2,000 per year for attending industry conferences, including registration, travel, and accommodation.

### 6.3 Learning Resources
Free access to:
- O'Reilly Learning (books, courses, live events)
- LinkedIn Learning
- Internal lunch-and-learn sessions (monthly)
- Mentorship program (formal matching twice per year)

## 7. Additional Perks

## 8. Benefits Enrollment

### 8.1 Open Enrollment
Open enrollment occurs annually during the first two weeks of November. Changes take effect January 1.

### 8.2 Qualifying Life Events
You may make benefits changes outside of open enrollment within 30 days of a qualifying life event:
- Marriage or divorce
- Birth or adoption of a child
- Loss of other coverage
- Relocation affecting plan networks

### 8.3 How to Enroll
1. Log in to the HR Portal (BambooHR)
2. Navigate to "Benefits" → "Enrollment"
3. Review plan options and make selections
4. Confirm elections and designate beneficiaries
5. Save and print confirmation for your records
