# Acme Corporation New Employee Onboarding Guide
**Version 4.0 | Effective Date: January 1, 2025 | Department: Human Resources**
**Access Level: All Employees | Owner: People Operations Team**

---

## 1. Welcome and First Day

### 1.1 Before Your First Day
Your onboarding coordinator will send you a Welcome Package email 5 business days before your start date, which includes:
- Login credentials for email and HR portal
- IT equipment request form
- Benefits enrollment instructions
- First-day schedule and office directions
- Emergency contact form
- Direct deposit enrollment form
- Tax forms (W-4, state withholding)

### 1.2 First Day Schedule
| Time | Activity | Location |
|---|---|---|
| 9:00 AM | Welcome and badge pickup | Reception / Lobby |
| 9:30 AM | HR orientation session | Conference Room A |
| 10:30 AM | IT setup and equipment | IT Help Desk |
| 11:30 AM | Office tour | Guided by onboarding buddy |
| 12:00 PM | Welcome lunch with team | Team's choice |
| 1:00 PM | Manager 1-on-1 | Manager's office |
| 2:00 PM | Department introduction | Team area |
| 3:00 PM | Self-paced setup and reading | Your desk |
| 4:00 PM | End-of-day check-in with buddy | Informal |

### 1.3 Onboarding Buddy
Every new employee is assigned an onboarding buddy from their team. Your buddy will:
- Show you around the office
- Introduce you to key team members
- Answer day-to-day questions
- Check in with you weekly for your first 30 days

## 2. Week 1 Checklist

Complete the following items during your first week:

- [ ] Complete all tax and payroll forms via HR portal
- [ ] Set up direct deposit
- [ ] Enroll in benefits (health, dental, vision, 401k)
- [ ] Complete required compliance training modules
- [ ] Set up your development environment (engineering roles)
- [ ] Schedule 1-on-1 meetings with direct teammates
- [ ] Review the Employee Handbook
- [ ] Review the Code of Conduct
- [ ] Set up Slack profile and join required channels
- [ ] Complete security awareness training
- [ ] Set up VPN access if working remotely

## 3. First 30 Days

### 3.1 Training Requirements
All new employees must complete the following training within 30 days:

| Training Module | Duration | Format |
|---|---|---|
| Company Culture & Values | 1 hour | Online |
| Security Awareness | 45 minutes | Online |
| Anti-Harassment Training | 1 hour | Online |
| Data Privacy (GDPR/CCPA) | 30 minutes | Online |
| Emergency Procedures | 30 minutes | In-person |
| Role-Specific Training | Varies | Manager-led |

### 3.2 30-Day Check-In
Your manager will conduct a formal 30-day check-in to:
- Review your onboarding experience
- Discuss initial performance expectations
- Identify any additional support or resources needed
- Set preliminary goals for the next 60 days

## 4. First 90 Days (Probation Period)

### 4.1 Probation Overview
All new employees are subject to a 90-day probation period. During this time:
- Performance will be evaluated monthly
- A formal 90-day review will determine continuation of employment
- PTO may be used after 30 days of employment
- Benefits coverage begins on the first day of the month following your start date

### 4.2 90-Day Review
The 90-day review covers:
- Job performance and quality of work
- Cultural fit and teamwork
- Completion of required training
- Goal achievement
- Mutual feedback on the onboarding experience

### 4.3 Post-Probation
Upon successful completion of the probation period:
- You will receive a confirmation email from HR
- Your employment status changes from "Probationary" to "Regular"
- You become eligible for the employee referral program
- Stock option vesting schedule begins (if applicable)

## 7. FAQ for New Employees

**Q: When do my benefits start?**
A: Benefits coverage begins on the first day of the month following your start date. For example, if you start on January 15, coverage begins February 1.

**Q: How do I request time off?**
A: Submit PTO requests through the HR portal. You can start using PTO after your first 30 days.

**Q: What if I forget my password?**
A: Contact the IT Help Desk at it-help@acmecorp.example.com or extension 4357 for password resets.

**Q: Where do I find company policies?**
A: All policies are available on the internal wiki (Confluence/Notion) under the "HR Policies" section, or you can ask the HR chatbot.

**Q: Who do I talk to if I have a concern about my manager or team?**
A: You can speak to HR directly, contact the Ethics Hotline, or submit an anonymous report through the HR portal.
