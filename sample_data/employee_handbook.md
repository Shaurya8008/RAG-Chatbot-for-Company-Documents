# Acme Corporation Employee Handbook
**Version 3.2 | Effective Date: January 1, 2025 | Department: Human Resources**
**Access Level: All Employees | Owner: HR Department**

---

## 1. Welcome to Acme Corporation

Welcome to Acme Corporation! We are thrilled to have you join our team. This handbook is designed to provide you with important information about working at Acme, including our policies, benefits, and workplace expectations.

Acme Corporation was founded in 2010 and has grown to over 500 employees across 4 offices. Our mission is to deliver innovative solutions that transform how businesses operate.

## 2. Employment Policies

### 2.1 Equal Employment Opportunity
Acme Corporation is committed to providing equal employment opportunities to all employees and applicants. We do not discriminate based on race, color, religion, sex, national origin, age, disability, genetic information, veteran status, or any other legally protected characteristic.

### 2.2 At-Will Employment
Employment at Acme Corporation is at-will, meaning either the employee or the company may terminate the employment relationship at any time, with or without cause or notice, subject to applicable law.

### 2.3 Employment Categories
- **Full-Time**: Employees regularly scheduled to work 40 hours per week
- **Part-Time**: Employees regularly scheduled to work less than 30 hours per week
- **Contract**: Employees hired for a specific project or time period
- **Intern**: Students or recent graduates in temporary learning positions

## 3. Work Hours and Attendance

### 3.1 Standard Work Hours
The standard workweek is Monday through Friday, 9:00 AM to 6:00 PM, with a one-hour lunch break. Core collaboration hours are 10:00 AM to 4:00 PM.

### 3.2 Flexible Work Arrangements
Acme supports flexible work arrangements including:
- **Hybrid Work**: Employees may work remotely up to 3 days per week with manager approval
- **Flexible Hours**: Start times between 7:00 AM and 10:00 AM are permitted with manager approval
- **Compressed Workweek**: Four 10-hour days may be arranged with department head approval

### 3.3 Remote Work Policy
Remote work eligibility is determined by role requirements and manager discretion. Remote employees must:
- Maintain a dedicated, secure workspace
- Be available during core collaboration hours (10 AM - 4 PM)
- Attend in-office days as required by their team
- Use company-approved VPN for all work activities

## 4. Compensation and Payroll

### 4.1 Pay Schedule
Employees are paid bi-weekly on every other Friday. If a payday falls on a holiday, payment will be issued on the preceding business day.

### 4.2 Direct Deposit
All employees are required to enroll in direct deposit within 30 days of hire. Forms are available from the HR portal.

### 4.3 Overtime
Non-exempt employees are eligible for overtime pay at 1.5 times their regular hourly rate for hours worked beyond 40 in a workweek. All overtime must be pre-approved by the employee's manager.

### 4.4 Performance Reviews
Performance reviews are conducted annually in Q1. Mid-year check-ins occur in July. Compensation adjustments, if any, take effect on April 1st following the annual review.

## 5. Workplace Conduct

### 5.1 Professional Behavior
All employees are expected to maintain professional conduct that reflects Acme's values of integrity, respect, collaboration, and innovation.

### 5.2 Anti-Harassment Policy
Acme Corporation maintains a zero-tolerance policy for harassment of any kind. This includes sexual harassment, bullying, intimidation, and any behavior that creates a hostile work environment. All reports will be investigated promptly and confidentially.

### 5.3 Conflict of Interest
Employees must disclose any potential conflicts of interest to their manager and HR. Outside employment or business activities that may conflict with Acme's interests require written approval from the department head.

### 5.4 Confidentiality
All employees must protect confidential company information, trade secrets, and proprietary data. This obligation continues after employment ends.

## 6. Technology and Security

### 6.1 Acceptable Use
Company technology resources including computers, email, internet, and software are provided for business use. Limited personal use is permitted if it does not interfere with work responsibilities.

### 6.2 Data Security
Employees must:
- Use strong, unique passwords and enable multi-factor authentication
- Lock screens when away from workstations
- Report suspected security incidents to IT immediately
- Not install unauthorized software on company devices
- Follow data classification and handling guidelines

### 6.3 Social Media Policy
Employees may use personal social media but must not share confidential company information, speak on behalf of the company without authorization, or post content that violates company policies.

## 7. Grievance Procedure

### 7.1 Reporting Issues
Employees who have workplace concerns should:
1. Discuss the issue with their direct manager
2. If unresolved, escalate to the department head
3. If still unresolved, contact HR directly
4. For serious matters (harassment, discrimination, safety), contact HR immediately

### 7.2 Whistleblower Protection
Acme prohibits retaliation against any employee who reports concerns in good faith. Reports can be made anonymously through the Ethics Hotline at ethics@acmecorp.example.com.
